Intro

You are going about your business one day, sitting on the couch playing video games, when a terrible feeling overcomes you.

"I'm hungry... I'm angry... I'm HANGRY!!!", you growl.

You look around, but can't find any satisfactory snacks. Just when you are ready to resign yourself to your fate, a magical imp appears.

"Ooh hoo hoo hoo! What's this I see? Are you looking for a snack? Answer my riddles, and you'll get what you seek!" cackles the imp, while hopping around in some sort of annoying dance.

You think to yourself why you have to go through all this trouble, but that you don't really have anything else to do anyway, so why not?
